A Green-Onion Pancake Recipe that is Dangerously Delicious

There are many, many foods that I obesses over. I mean, MANY. I moved to Seattle to work on a Taiwanese food truck and it was there I became a master at making these savory pancakes. They are savory and sweet and will give you something to look forward to each workday.

  • 2 cups APF
  • 3/4 cup cold water
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 6 stalks green onion
  • Vegetable or Canola Oil
  • tsp five spice powder
  • Hoisin (for dipping)
